## VOICE & TONE

### Brand Voice Characteristics

1. **Empowering** - Help users achieve their goals
2. **Professional** - Business-grade quality
3. **Accessible** - Clear, jargon-free language
4. **Innovative** - Cutting-edge but not intimidating

### Tone Guidelines

**DO:**
- Be direct and get to the point quickly
- Use active voice ("Create your website" not "Your website can be created")
- Keep sentences short (15-20 words max)
- Use "you" and "your" (talk directly to the user)
- Be confident but humble

**DON'T:**
- Use excessive exclamation marks!!!
- Over-promise ("Revolutionary! Game-changing! Mind-blowing!")
- Use jargon without explanation
- Write in passive voice
- Be overly casual or use slang

## STANDARD MESSAGING

### Hero Headlines (H1)

**Format:** Action + Benefit

**Good Examples:**
- "Create Professional Resumes in Minutes"
- "Generate Invoices That Get Paid Faster"
- "Build Presentations That Captivate"

**Bad Examples:**
- "The Ultimate Resume Builder" (generic)
- "Revolutionary AI Technology" (over-promise)
- "Resumes Made Easy" (vague)

### Subheadlines (H2)

**Format:** How It Works or Key Benefit

**Good Examples:**
- "AI-powered tools that understand your needs"
- "Professional results without the learning curve"
- "From idea to finished product in minutes"

**Bad Examples:**
- "Powered by GPT-4, Claude, and Gemini" (technical)
- "The best solution on the market" (unprovable)
- "Amazing features you'll love" (vague)

### Call-to-Action (CTA) Buttons

**Primary CTAs:**
- "Get Started Free"
- "Create Your [Item]"
- "Try It Now"
- "Start Building"

**Secondary CTAs:**
- "Learn More"
- "View Pricing"
- "See Examples"
- "Contact Sales"

**‚ùå Avoid:**
- "Click Here" (not descriptive)
- "Submit" (sounds like a chore)
- "Enter" (unclear)
- "Go" (too vague)

## FEATURE DESCRIPTIONS

### Format Rules

1. Start with benefit, then feature
2. Use specific numbers when possible
3. Keep under 15 words
4. Use active voice

**Good:**
- "Save 3 hours per week with automated content generation"
- "Export to PDF, PowerPoint, or share online instantly"
- "Unlimited revisions included in all plans"

**Bad:**
- "Content can be generated automatically" (passive)
- "Various export options available" (vague)
- "You can make changes" (obvious, not benefit)

## ACCESSIBILITY LANGUAGE

### Alt Text for Images

**Format:** [What it is] [What it shows] [Why it matters]

**Good:**
- "Screenshot of Javari Resume Builder showing a completed resume with professional formatting"
- "Javari AI avatar with welcoming gesture on the homepage"
- "Bar chart showing 50% time savings using automated invoicing"

**Bad:**
- "Image" (not descriptive)
- "Screenshot" (what's in it?)
- "Chart" (what kind? what data?)

### Link Text

**Good:**
- "View our pricing plans"
- "Download the complete guide"
- "Contact our sales team"

**Bad:**
- "Click here"
- "Read more"
- "Learn more" (without context)

## DOCUMENTATION LANGUAGE

### Writing Style for Docs

1. Use second person ("you")
2. Active voice only
3. Present tense
4. Short sentences (12-15 words average)
5. One idea per paragraph

**Example:**

**Good:**
```
To create your first resume:
1. Click "New Resume" in the dashboard
2. Choose a template
3. Fill in your information
4. Click "Generate" to see AI suggestions

Your resume appears in the preview pane. Make any edits you want, 
then download as PDF.
```

**Bad:**
```
Resumes can be created by navigating to the dashboard where the user 
will find various options including templates that should be selected 
based on preferences after which information can be entered...
```
